Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a service through Apna Garage? +
- After logging in, you can select a service category (e.g., mechanic, electrician, fuel supplier, or body technician). Choose the specific service you need, provide details, and a technician will be matched with your request.
What types of services are available on Apna Garage? +
- We offer a range of car services, including general maintenance, electrical repairs, bodywork, and emergency fuel delivery. Choose the type that fits your needs to view the available technicians.
How are technicians selected and vetted? +
- All technicians undergo a thorough background check and must meet certain skill and certification requirements to join Apna Garage. We ensure quality and reliability with every technician listed.
How do I know the cost of a service before booking? +
- Estimated costs are displayed when you select a service and provide details. Once you choose a technician, you’ll receive a final quote before confirming your booking.
Can Apna Garage help in roadside assistance? +
- Yes, Apna Garage offers roadside assistance for fuel delivery, general repairs, and more. Select “Roadside Assistance” when booking to get help from the nearest available technician.
